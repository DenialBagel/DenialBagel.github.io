Who am I

My name's Rachel. (YES IT'S MY REAL NAME) I was born and raised in Hong Kong. I have been working and travelling ever since I turned 18.

I love languages. I speak four (Cantonese, English, Mandarin, Spanish) and teach three (Cantonese, English, Mandarin).

I am a U56 JR powerlifter.

I am also a line cook. I have worked in restaurants, cafes, hotels. I have a love-hate relationship with this line of work but mostly I love what I do.

Half of the year I am a solo backpacker. As of the making of this website I have just come back from an epic adventure to Central America.
