Aim of this page

I'm documenting my language learning journey, as a motivation for me to keep going and hopefully as an inspiration to you guys to start your own learning project.

My projects

Video diary

I was doing a journaling exercise, and the idea suddenly occured to me that I can shoot a video of me talking about my day in Spanish.

So that's what I did. I shot a 7 minute video, using different corners of my bedroom, playing with different lighting. It turned out quite lovely. Watch it here. Later, I went on to making more videos regularly.

Spanish blog posts

I try writing in Spanish as much as I can, because writing is a very good exercise to train my ability to think in Spanish. Read my entries here.

Teaching projects

I started teaching Cantonese online since April 2025. This journey, although still in its infancy, has been incredibly fruitful. I have had the privilege of teaching students from all over the world, from toddlers to working adults.

In addition to private sessions, I made some Cantonese comprehensible input videos to facilitate my students' learning.
